TECHNICAL REFERENCE & WORKABILITY GUIDE

Slump Test Standards

For different types of construction, recommended slump values:

  • Columns & Walls: 75 - 125 mm
  • Beams & Slabs: 50 - 100 mm
  • Mass Concrete: 25 - 50 mm
  • Pavement: 25 - 40 mm

Curing Requirements

Standard curing time for M20 Grade:

  • OPC Cement: Min. 7 Days
  • PPC/Fly Ash: Min. 10 Days
  • Hot Weather: Min. 14 Days
Engineering Disclaimer

The calculations provided are based on nominal mix ratios as per IS 456:2000 and ACI standards. Actual field results may vary based on aggregate moisture content, silt in sand, and specific gravity of materials. It is recommended to perform a Trial Mix before large scale casting.
