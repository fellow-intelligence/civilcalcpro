CRITICAL SLAB POURING CHECKLIST (PRE-CONCRETING)

Formwork & Shuttering

  • Alignment: Ensure formwork is perfectly leveled and water-tight.
  • Support: Check all props and jacks for stability under wet concrete load.
  • Oiling: Apply shuttering oil for easy removal and smooth finish.

Reinforcement (Steel)

  • Cover Blocks: Ensure 20mm-25mm clear cover for slab bottom steel.
  • Chairs: Use proper 'chairs' to maintain the gap between top and bottom mesh.
  • Tying: Verify all intersections are tightly bound with GI wire.
Important Pouring Note

Always ensure a Slump Test is conducted before pouring. For standard slabs, a slump of 50mm to 100mm is recommended to ensure proper compaction and workability.
