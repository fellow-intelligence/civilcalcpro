TECHNICAL REFERENCE & QUALITY GUIDE

Field Tests for Blocks

  • Visual Check: Blocks should be free from cracks, honeycomb, and chipped edges.
  • Dimensions: Tolerance should not exceed ±3mm in length and ±2mm in height.
  • Soundness: A clear ringing sound should be produced when two blocks are struck.

Installation Guide

  • Pre-Wetting: Do not soak hollow blocks; slightly dampen the surface only.
  • Joints: Maintain uniform 10mm (3/8") joints for both horizontal and vertical.
  • Curing: Water curing should be done for a minimum of 7 to 10 days.
Engineering Tip: Mortar Mix

For external or load-bearing hollow block walls, a 1:4 cement-sand mix is recommended. Ensure the mortar is plastic enough to fill the block webs for better structural bonding.
