PLASTERING TECHNICAL MANUAL & QUALITY GUIDE

Surface Preparation

  • Cleaning: Remove all dust, loose mortar, and oil from the wall.
  • Wetting: Soak the wall thoroughly with water 2 hours before plastering.
  • Hacking: For RCC surfaces, 300 hacks per sq.ft are recommended for bonding.

Curing & Finishing

  • Mixing Time: Dry mix for 2 mins, then add water and mix for 3 mins.
  • Layer Thickness: Single coat should not exceed 15mm (0.6 inches).
  • Curing: Keep the surface moist for at least 7 to 10 days.
Engineering Note on Ratios

Standard practice for internal walls is 1:4 (Cement:Sand), while for external walls or ceilings, a richer mix of 1:3 is preferred to prevent water seepage and ensure better adhesion.
