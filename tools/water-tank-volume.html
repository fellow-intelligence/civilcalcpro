TANK MAINTENANCE & HYGIENE CHECKLIST

Cleaning Standards

  • Frequency: Potable water tanks should be cleaned and disinfected every 6 months.
  • Sediment Removal: Drain the tank fully and remove all sludge/silt from the floor.
  • Chlorination: Use a 50-100 ppm chlorine solution to scrub the internal walls.

Structural Integrity

  • Seepage Check: Inspect external walls for dampness or saltpetre (shora) formation.
  • Ventilation: Ensure air vents are screened with fine mesh to prevent insect entry.
  • Lid Security: The manhole cover must be air-tight and elevated to prevent rainwater inflow.
Engineering Note: Water Weight

Remember that water is heavy (1000kg per m³). Overhead tanks must be supported by structural beams designed to handle the Full Wet Load plus a 20% safety factor.
