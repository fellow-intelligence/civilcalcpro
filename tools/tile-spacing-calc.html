PROFESSIONAL TILING INSTALLATION CHECKLIST

Surface Preparation

  • Substrate Leveling: Ensure the floor or wall is perfectly flat (±3mm over 2m).
  • Priming: Apply a suitable primer to porous surfaces to prevent rapid water absorption from thinset.
  • Cleaning: Remove all dust, oil, and debris for maximum adhesive bond.

Installation Quality

  • Adhesive Coverage: Ensure at least 95% coverage on the back of each tile.
  • Spacers: Use spacers consistently to maintain perfectly straight grout lines.
  • Grouting: Only apply grout after the adhesive has cured for at least 24 hours.
Engineering Note: Expansion Joints

For large tiled areas, include expansion joints every 5 to 8 meters to prevent cracking caused by structural movement or thermal expansion.
