TECHNICAL REFERENCE & QUALITY GUIDE

Field Tests for Bricks

  • Hardness: Scratching with finger nail should leave no mark.
  • Soundness: Two bricks striking should produce a clear metallic ringing.
  • Absorption: Should not absorb more than 20% water by dry weight.

Curing & Strength

  • Standard Curing: Minimum 7-10 Days is required[cite: 74, 75].
  • Joint Thickness: Standard joint should be 10mm (3/8").
  • Soaking: Soak bricks in water for 2 hours before use.
Engineering Disclaimer [cite: 78]

The calculations are based on standard nominal mix ratios as per IS 456:2000[cite: 79]. Actual quantities may vary based on brick size tolerances and mortar wastage on site[cite: 80]. A trial batch is recommended for large masonry works[cite: 81].
