STRUCTURAL DESIGN & COMPLIANCE GUIDE

Limit State Checks

  • Flexural Strength: Ensure the design moment ($M_u$) is less than the nominal moment capacity ($\phi M_n$).
  • Shear Capacity: Provided shear reinforcement must handle the factored shear force at the supports.
  • Deflection: Check mid-span deflection ($\Delta_{max} = \frac{5wL^4}{384EI}$) against span/360 limits.

Professional Standards

  • ACI 318: Standard requirements for reinforced concrete beam design.
  • AISC 360: Specifications for structural steel buildings.
  • Symmetry: UDL on a simply supported beam results in symmetric stress distribution.
Engineering Alert: Instability

If the span-to-depth ratio exceeds 20, the beam may be susceptible to excessive vibration and lateral-torsional buckling. Ensure lateral bracing is considered for steel sections.
